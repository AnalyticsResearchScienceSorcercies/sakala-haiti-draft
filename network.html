Two clusters lead the network... three TapTaps in the Nord, one in Grand'Anse. The rest fan out across ten departments.

The active network

Ten TapTaps are working today across seven departments, 160 youth in all. Beyond the four surveyed TapTaps, here are the rest... each ready to take on a sponsor and grow its cohort.

Grande Rivière du Nord · Bonamy

Nord · 20 youth

Strong nursery capacity, moringa and masketi. Regional hub potential.

Limonade · SONJE AYITI

Nord-Est · 30 youth

Largest active TapTap, medicinal plants with the SONJE AYITI NGO, next to the Henry Christophe TapTap. Natural home for the distillery.

Limonade · Bois de Lance

Nord-Est · 10 youth

Moringa and masketi, near the Henry Christophe university TapTap.

Plaine du Nord · OZONE

Nord · 10 youth

Institutional partner on the Cap-Haïtien corridor. Processing-hub candidate.

Cité Soleil · SAKALA HQ

Ouest · 25 youth

Headquarters. Moringa and medicinal plants. Dan's 20-year community trust base.

Cité Soleil · Sarthe

Ouest · 10 youth

Moringa nursery. Networks with HQ for shared processing.

The Border Farm (faith-led)

Haiti / DR border · ~200 acres

A priest farming two hundred acres, already running honey and cassava. Returning youth need a reason to stay. Awaiting GPS survey.

The Youth Rabbitry

North Haiti · staff in place

Trained youth, staff, pens and stock ready. Paused only because its last funding ended... the fastest to restart.

Reactivation pipeline

Fifteen more TapTaps went dormant when funding lapsed... proven community ties and youth networks, ready to restart at a fraction of greenfield cost.
