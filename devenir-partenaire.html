TapTap Hosts

Host a
TapTap

For organizations, landowners, and committed citizens

Together, Let's Build a More United Future for Haiti.

Are you a farmland owner, a business leader, a member of a community organization, or simply a committed citizen? KONKRET invites you to host a TapTap by putting your land, ideas, or facilities to work for Haiti's youth.

Partnering with KONKRET takes no capital. It takes intention. Together we build the conditions for young people to work, learn, and move forward... on your land, in your workshop, around your business.

What You Gain

Why Host a TapTap

01
Put Your Resources to Work
Unused land, empty workshops, underused spaces... your assets become a lever for local development.
02
Create Jobs
Every partnership creates real jobs for young Haitians who have no other way into the formal economy.
03
Strengthen Your Impact
Your name and your organization are tied to a recognized initiative. Stronger network, visibility, and local standing.
04
Full Support
Technical guidance, a partner network, and our team on the ground at every step of the partnership.
Ways to Partner

How to Work Together

Provide Farmland

Have unused land? KONKRET places a team of young people on it, handles the management, and shares the proceeds under a clear agreement.

Partner with a Business or Cooperative

You run a business and want to bring in trained young people within a structured, secure setup.

Host Internships or Jobs

Offer an internship or an entry-level position to a young person mentored by KONKRET. Ideal for institutions, offices, and training organizations.

Logistical or Material Support

Tools, supplies, materials, or services... Your in-kind contribution can transform the productivity of an entire site.

Lodging or Training Space

A room, a hall, an outdoor space... Make a place available for our training sessions or cooperative meetings.

Pilot Project or Solidarity Initiative

Have an idea, a concept, an experience to share? KONKRET can turn it into a pilot project that can be repeated in other areas.

My land was just an empty field. Today it's farmed by a team of motivated young people, and it supports several families. I had nothing to manage... KONKRET took care of everything.
