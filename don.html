Donate

Tax-deductible giving

Through SAKALA International, a 501(c)(3) organization in the United States. For donors and organizations who want to give directly... cash or in-kind.

Most of what we do isn't charity. You sponsor a TapTap or buy the harvest, and the youth keep the wages and the gold. But some supporters... churches, foundations, companies... would rather give a tax-deductible gift. This is that door. Every gift routes through SAKALA International (501c3), is tracked on the public ledger, and comes back to you with a receipt for your taxes.

Cash gift

Give funds

A tax-deductible financial gift to SAKALA International. One-time or recurring. Directed to youth employment on the ground, or left unrestricted for the network to place where it's needed most.

In-kind gift

Give equipment or materials

Tools, agricultural equipment, processing machinery, vehicles, solar, building materials, supplies... donated in-kind and deductible at fair market value. Tell us what you have and we'll coordinate delivery and issue your receipt.

Fill the form and our team confirms fit, logistics, and value within 72 hours, then documents the donation for your records.
