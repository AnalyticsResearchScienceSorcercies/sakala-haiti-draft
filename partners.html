Who Stands Behind the Work

Partners

Ownership is a team sport. These are the people and institutions who backed KONKRET before it was easy... a research partner, women religious, a compost partner, diaspora parishes, and the Haitian diaspora itself.

The Research Partner
Data · Strategy · Research

BARSS

Bertil's Analytics Research Sciences & Sorceries. The economist who found the work, joined it, and set out to prove it. BARSS traces, dollar by dollar, whether money becomes economic power on the ground or leaks on the way down... the discipline that turns a good program into one that can go to scale. It is the quiet firm that helps organizations doing real work turn it into gold, and rarely takes the credit. Here, it takes it.
