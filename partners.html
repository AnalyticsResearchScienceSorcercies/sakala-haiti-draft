Who already backs this

Partners

KONKRET does not work alone. The organizations below already stand behind the work... some funding cohorts, some with hands in the soil, some carrying the harvest. This is who makes it real. The open question is simple: will you be the next one?

Founding partner of peace

Sisters of St. Joseph of Peace

Present in Haiti's North since 2009, through their mission at Hôpital Sacré Coeur in Milot. The JOB FOR PEACE partnership stands behind a simple conviction: the most direct answer to violence is a young person with work, skills, and a place to belong.

A recognition... read it
Operating partner

Impact 480 / Fatraka

The social-enterprise arm founded by Daniel Tillias, carrying years of ground credibility in the North. The day-to-day operating partner across KONKRET's programs.

Ecological partner

SOIL

Ecological sanitation and compost in Cap-Haïtien. A working partner on the soil loop that feeds the gardens and on community water and sanitation.

oursoil.org
Energy partner

Okra Solar

Solar electrification for SAKALA campuses... clean power for the cooperative's work, with full due diligence complete.

okrasolar.com
Campus neighbor

Hôpital Sacré Coeur, Milot

The hospital in Milot, neighbor to SAKALA's northern campus... the same town where the Sisters' Haiti mission began. Adjacency that keeps the work rooted in one community.

US nonprofit arm

SAKALA International

The United States nonprofit that carries the 23-year community-center story to donors abroad and channels their support back to the ground in Haiti.
