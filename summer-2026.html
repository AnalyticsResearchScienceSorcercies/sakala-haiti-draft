The Program

Eight Weeks To Change A Destiny

SAKALA is KONKRET's intensive agricultural apprenticeship program. For 8 weeks, the young people of Milot and the surrounding area work side by side on 21 acres of land at Grand Bassin... technical training, one-on-one mentoring, a regular wage, and a certificate at the end.

This is not an internship. It is a cooperative in motion. Every participant learns to grow, to manage, to work together... and leaves with real skills, real experience, and a strong network.

The Summer 2026 program is the first phase of a model designed to support 50,000 electrical connections in the Milot region in partnership with BARSS.

Nou plante kè kontan, nou rekòlte lavi. We plant with joy, we harvest life.

Skills

The 8 Apprenticeship Tracks

Every participant chooses a main track and builds cross-cutting skills. Versatility is the rule.

01
Market Gardening
Vegetable growing, crop rotation, soil management.
02
Nursery
Raising seedlings, sowing, caring for young shoots.
03
Irrigation
Water management, smart watering, saving water.
04
Agroforestry
Fruit trees, ground cover, reforestation.
05
Processing
Adding value to the harvest, preserving, finished products.
06
Management
Simple bookkeeping, cost pricing, cooperative management.
07
Leadership
Communication, conflict resolution, teamwork.
08
Marketing & Sales
Direct selling, local markets, buyer relationships.
