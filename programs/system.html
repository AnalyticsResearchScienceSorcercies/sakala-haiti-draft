The Unified
Architecture:
How They Connect

Eight programs. One trust. All feeding the same gold reserve. The fractal Konbit is not a metaphor. It is a governance structure.

Each of the eight programs operates under the same three-layer architecture: the Fon Kominote SAKALA master trust (40% of every surplus dollar, irrevocable, converted to Lò SAKALA gold), the cooperative wage structure (40%, scaled by certification tier), and the operational reinvestment pool (20%, funds the next franchise or next cohort). The programs are independent enterprises. The gold reserve is shared.

The Fon Kominote SAKALA

The master trust is a Delaware irrevocable charitable trust. It holds six sub-trusts, one per cooperative enterprise: Pratik Trash Gold Trust, Pratik Rabbit Gold Trust, Pratik Reforestation Gold Trust, Konbit Press Gold Trust, Plakèt Directory Gold Trust, Plakèt Payroll Gold Trust. Each sub-trust holds its own Lò SAKALA allocation. Each sub-trust pays out to the cooperative members of its own enterprise. No cross-contamination. No shared liability. One roof.

Quarterly allocation formula: (tier_multiplier × contribution_units) ÷ Σ(tier_multiplier × contribution_units). The tier system solves the incentive problem in cooperative structures. Long-tenure, high-certification workers earn disproportionately more from the same pool. New entrants earn the floor, not zero. The system does not require anyone to trust anyone. The math enforces the incentive.

The Three-Entity Firewall

SAKALA Community Center

23-year NGO. Existing donors protected. Youth programs, Cité Soleil, job-for-peace mandate. Separate books. Separate governance. Does not participate in the commercial JV. Contracts vendor services at fair-market rates only.

Fatraka + Impact 480 LLC

Daniel Tillias's operational vehicles. Fatraka handles waste logistics; Impact 480 is the JV member. The commercial revenue engine lives here, firewalled from SAKALA Community Center's donor relationships. Clean separation of accounts.

BARSS LLC

Wesley Bertil's research and IP entity. Provides the EEDTM framework, analytics, the Pratik tech stack (Treasury, Hub, Subscriber Portal, BIP), and the US front office. Licenses methodology. No external hire cost. Deployed as-is.

The JV (Perpetual)

Impact 480 LLC (50%) + BARSS LLC (50%). Delaware perpetual LLC. No sunset. No forced buyout. 8 locked foundational decisions: 50/50 split, perpetual term, co-management, reciprocal lifetime stipend, cross-guaranty, Delaware formation, 3-way firewall, 3-bucket waterfall.
