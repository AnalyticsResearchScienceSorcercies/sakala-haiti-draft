For Young People

I Want
To Work

For young Haitians aged 16 to 20

Join the Konbit: a job, training, mentorship... and a real chance to build your life.

The land as the engine, youth as the force. KONKRET doesn't just give you a paycheck... KONKRET gives you a future. Since 2019, we have organized jobs for young Haitians in the rural areas of Haiti, placing them on structured partner farms and supporting them every step of the way.

What You Gain

Your Benefits

01
Steady Pay
Secure payment every week or every month, depending on the agreement with the partner farm.
02
Meals On Site
Depending on the partner farm, one meal a day is provided at the workplace.
03
Learning
Building farming and personal skills throughout your journey.
04
Certificate
A certificate of participation... a real asset for your professional future.
05
A Family
You join a network that supports you. A konbit... a community moving forward together.
To Join

The Requirements

  • Be between 16 and 20 years old
  • Be motivated, punctual, and ready to work as a team
  • Be willing to be placed on a partner farm in your area
  • Take part in a short intake interview (spoken or written)
The Process
  1. Fill out the enrollment form
  2. Spoken or written interview with our team
  3. Placement on a partner farm in your area
  4. Start working

We receive applications every week. Reserve your spot now.
